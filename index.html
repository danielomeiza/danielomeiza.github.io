Biography

I am currently undertaking doctoral research in the Computer Science Department at the University of Oxford. I am advised by Marina Jirotka and Lars Kunze in the Human Centred Computing group and the Cognitive Robotics group. My PhD research takes an interdisciplinary form in that I build on theories in explainable AI and in behavioural sciences to design explanation techniques that can provide clear and intelligible explanations for the decisions of AI systems to stakeholders. This involves the use of both quantitative and quantitative approaches. My research experience and findings so far are applicable in other domains e.g., in autonomous driving, finance, healthcare, assistive technology, and it is generously funded by RoboTIPS and SAX.
